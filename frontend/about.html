About Aagaz Foundation

Founded in 2020, Aagaz Foundation is a registered, non-profit organisation committed to empowering communities across Bihar through sustainable development initiatives.

Headquartered in Surbhi Vihar, Bhupatipur, Patna (800020), with a district office in Akhtiyarpur, Muzaffarpur, the foundation works towards holistic social upliftment by focusing on women empowerment, education, health, and livelihood generation.

From the Chairman's Desk

At Aagaz Foundation, we believe that true development begins when opportunities reach every corner of society—especially the rural and marginalised sections.

Our journey started with a simple yet powerful vision: to empower individuals through education, skill development, health awareness, and livelihood support. Over the years, we have seen how small efforts can create lasting transformations. Every woman who learns a new skill, every youth who gains employment, and every child who steps into a classroom represents a step forward towards a stronger Bihar.

"Our dedicated team and partners, including ABS Manufacturing Vastra Nirman Udyog and Budhinath Traders Private Limited, have been instrumental in turning our vision into reality. Together, we continue to work tirelessly to build communities that are self-reliant, confident, and capable of shaping their own future."

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and uplift the underprivileged communities of Bihar by promoting education, health, and self-employment. We aim to create sustainable opportunities for women and youth through skill development, awareness programmes, and community participation.

Aagaz Foundation is committed to fostering equality, self-reliance, and social responsibility—ensuring that every individual has the tools and confidence to lead a dignified and independent life.

  • Driving Inclusive Growth

Who We Serve

Aagaz Foundation works at the grassroots level to serve women, youth, and underprivileged communities across Bihar. Our initiatives focus on empowering those who are often left behind in the journey of development.

We serve:
  • Women seeking skill development, self-employment, and financial independence.
  • Youth in need of education, career guidance, and vocational training for sustainable livelihoods.
  • Children from rural and low-income families, ensuring access to quality education and health awareness.
  • Rural communities aiming to improve their overall quality of life through collective growth and social participation.
